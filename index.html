What is Hacking?

Hacking is the form of taking controll of a computer sytem (eg website/ database), often to steal credit-card details. Hacking is a very common crime, and frightens many governments. Are there any good forms of hacking?

Though it may seem as a stupid idea, but can hacking actually be a good thing? Some groups are based on hacking terrorism, which helps the general public. One of the groups is called Anonymous, which has hacked ISIS and various other terroist organisiations. There are hackers which don't steal! These are reffered to as "White Hat Hackers", and they are one of many "major site's" biggest tools. These hacker look for flaws in the computer systems, but don't infultarte the website itself. These hackers notify the company, causing a reward to be given to the hackers. Belive it or not, "White Hat Hackers" can earn $62,000 per year (and thats starting salary)! Thats only the "White Hat Hackers" who have a official job, those who don't have even more profit. Some hackers can earn up to $10,000 for a single job. It's all law-biding too, making it a way to earn money without beign arrested.
